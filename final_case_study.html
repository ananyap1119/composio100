Limitations

Needs verification means insufficient public evidence, not missing functionality. Login-gated developer portals could not always be confirmed. Credential issuance and multi-tenant distribution were frequently unclear. Only decision-ready records are presented as decisive outcomes. Review-required records need human checking before operational use. Results reflect publicly accessible documentation at research time.
